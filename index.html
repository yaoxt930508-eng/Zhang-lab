About

这类“公开网站”应该传达什么

About the lab

An outward-facing site should explain who you are and why your work matters.

公开的实验室网站不只是展示研究方向，更是在向外界说明你们是谁、正在做什么、哪些成果对外开放、别人怎样与团队合作，以及新人怎样加入。 A public academic website is more than a profile page. It should make the lab understandable to students, collaborators, journalists, and researchers who discover your work for the first time.

所以这一版设计在视觉上更接近正式机构主页，在内容结构上则吸收了开源社区的组织方式，例如仓库入口、数据集链接、贡献说明和可复用资源。 That is why this version emphasizes editorial clarity: one leading story, well-labeled sections, restrained typography, and content blocks that feel close to a research publisher's homepage.
